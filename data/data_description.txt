This dataset contains SMS messages labeled as either 'spam' or 'ham'.

Column Descriptions:
- label: Target variable indicating whether a message is spam or ham.
- message: The actual text of the SMS message.

Original source: https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.kaggle.com%2Fdatasets%2Fuciml%2Fsms-spam-collection-dataset
